# 模特展示模式 - 简化为走路展示

## 📋 更新概览

将模特展示模式简化为 **模特自然走路展示**，去除复杂的镜头切换和特写，保持 **9:16 竖屏**格式，完美适配抖音、快手、小红书等短视频平台。

---

## 🎯 解决的问题

### 1. ✅ 全身展示不完整
**问题：** 视频只展示身体中间，头和腿被裁剪

**解决方案：**
- 强制要求 "COMPLETE full body shot from HEAD to FEET"
- 阶段 1 和阶段 4 都必须展示完整的头到脚
- 明确禁止裁剪头部、腿部或脚部

### 2. ✅ 手提包掉落问题
**问题：** 视频中部手提包莫名掉落，违背物理规律

**解决方案：**
- 新增 "ACCESSORIES CONSISTENCY" 强制规则
- 要求 "Handbags MUST stay firmly held - they CANNOT fall, drop, or disappear"
- 模特必须 "maintain firm, natural grip throughout"
- 物理一致性规则：NO objects falling unexpectedly

### 3. ✅ 服装变化问题
**问题：** 视频结尾服装完全变了，与原图不一致

**解决方案：**
- 新增 "CLOTHING CONSISTENCY (MANDATORY)" 最高优先级规则
- 要求 "The outfit MUST remain EXACTLY as shown - ZERO changes"
- 严格禁止添加、删除或修改任何服装元素
- 阶段 4 明确要求 "SAME EXACT outfit as at the beginning"

### 4. ✅ 横屏改竖屏
**问题：** 原来是 16:9 横屏，不适合短视频平台

**解决方案：**
- 改为 **9:16 竖屏**格式 (1080×1920)
- 完美适配抖音、快手、小红书、Instagram Reels
- 竖屏构图更适合展示模特全身

### 5. ✅ 简化为走路展示 🆕
**问题：** 复杂的镜头切换（远景→中景→特写→回到远景）容易导致服装变化

**解决方案：**
- 简化为 **8 秒连续走路展示**
- 全程保持完整全身镜头（头到脚）
- 无镜头切换，无特写，无复杂运镜
- 模特自然走路，展示服装在运动中的真实效果
- 更简单、更自然、更不容易出错

### 1. lib/prompts.ts

**关键修改：**

```typescript
// 新增最高优先级规则
CRITICAL REQUIREMENTS - ABSOLUTE RULES:

1. CLOTHING CONSISTENCY (MANDATORY):
   - The outfit MUST remain EXACTLY as shown
   - ZERO changes to clothing design, color, pattern, or style
   - NO adding/removing any clothing items

2. ACCESSORIES CONSISTENCY (MANDATORY):
   - Handbags MUST stay firmly held - CANNOT fall or drop
   - Jewelry must stay in place
   - Physical consistency throughout

3. FULL BODY VISIBILITY (MANDATORY):
   - ALWAYS show COMPLETE model from HEAD to FEET
   - NO cropping off head, legs, or feet

// 简化为走路展示 🆕
8-SECOND VIDEO STRUCTURE - SIMPLE WALKING SHOWCASE:

ENTIRE VIDEO (0-8 seconds) - Continuous Natural Walking:
- Model walks naturally in a straight line
- COMPLETE full body visible from HEAD to FEET throughout
- NO camera zooms, NO close-ups, NO complex movements
- Just simple, natural walking to show the outfit

// 视频方向改为竖屏
VIDEO ORIENTATION:
- VERTICAL 9:16 portrait format (1080x1920 pixels)
- Optimized for mobile viewing and social media

// 技术规格
TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS:
- Aspect Ratio: 9:16 VERTICAL portrait format (NOT 16:9)
- Resolution: 1080x1920 pixels
```
